FAQ

Does ZenTimer include guided meditations?

No. ZenTimer is intentionally a simple meditation timer without guided content.

Does ZenTimer use Apple Health?

Yes. Meditation sessions can sync to Apple Health for mindfulness history and stats continuity.

Is ZenTimer a social meditation app?

No social features are required. The app is built for private, distraction-free practice.
